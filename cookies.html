1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by websites you visit. They are generally used to keep websites working properly, remember preferences or collect anonymous usage data.

3. Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to manage or delete cookies through their settings. You can usually:

  • Delete existing cookies.
  • Block cookies from certain sites.
