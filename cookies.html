What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device that help websites function properly, understand performance, and improve user experience.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential cookies — Required for basic website operation.
  • Performance/Analytics — Minimal tracking (if enabled later) to improve navigation and content.
  • Functional cookies — Remember language selection or preferences.
  • Third-party cookies — Only added if we integrate tools like YouTube or Analytics.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to ensure smooth navigation and language display. We DO NOT use aggressive tracking or sell/share personal data.

Your Choices

You can:

  • Use the site without consenting to non-essential cookies.
  • Clear cookies anytime in browser settings.
  • Block cookies manually if preferred.

Future Changes

If we add Analytics, AI tracking, or marketing integrations in the future, this page will be updated clearly and transparently. You will always have the choice to opt-out.
